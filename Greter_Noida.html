Galgotias University

Galgotias University is a leading private university in Greater Noida, India.

It offers a wide range of courses in engineering, business, law, and humanities.

Known for innovation and campus life, it attracts thousands of students each year.

Sharda University

Sharda University is a globally recognized institution offering education across various disciplines.

It is known for its international student community and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Located in Knowledge Park III, it emphasizes innovation and interdisciplinary learning.

Gautam Buddha University

Gautam Buddha University is a government-funded university established by the UP Government.

It has a large eco-friendly campus offering programs in management, engineering, and social sciences.

The university promotes research, social development, and holistic education.

NIET (Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology)

NIET is an AICTE-approved private institute focused on engineering, pharmacy, and management courses.

It is known for excellent placement records, tech-driven education, and industry tie-ups.

Located in Knowledge Park II, NIET ranks among the top engineering colleges in UP.
